Make a contribution or raise an issue


Pre-requisite - To contribute you will need a GitHub Account. They are free and pretty quick to set up. If you have one, login.

Step 1 - Click here here to create a new issue describing your contribution.

Step 2 - Fill in the details and give it a label (missing word or bad description).

Step 3 - Wait for it to be approved. Chase the site managers if you have found their secret identity.

Step 4 - Bask in the glory of contribution and tell all your friends.
